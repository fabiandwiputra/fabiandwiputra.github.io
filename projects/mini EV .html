Mini EV Conversion

  • Tech Stack: Electric Motor, Battery Pack, Motor Controller, Chassis Modification
  • Github URL: project link

The Mini EV Conversion project involves converting a small gasoline-powered vehicle into an electric vehicle (EV). This conversion is an eco-friendly initiative aimed at reducing emissions and fuel consumption by replacing the internal combustion engine with an electric powertrain.

This project covers essential EV components and installation techniques, providing practical experience in electric mobility solutions and basic automotive electrical engineering.

Key Components:

  • Electric Motor: Replaces the internal combustion engine and provides propulsion.
  • Battery Pack: Stores electrical energy to power the motor.
  • Motor Controller: Regulates the power delivered to the motor.
  • Charger: Recharges the battery pack from an external power source.
  • Throttle and Wiring: Controls motor speed and connects all electrical components.
  • Modified Chassis: Adjusted to accommodate EV components safely and securely.
